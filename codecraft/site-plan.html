Purpose

I am proposing a portfolio website. I want to have a site that tells potential employers and others my skills. I will use this site for any future job applications and resumes.

My vision is for a great HOME page that is very eye catching and simple that lists my skills and the languages I know in tech.

My second page I want to have a great CONTACT ME page with a form to fill out, and my business email address. I could use this page to list my resume, and past employers.

My third page will be for PROJECTS. I would love to showcase some of the projects I’ve done in the past for web and also other coding projects such as in python, etc. I will figure out a way to develop a nice image text box and repeat it so it makes sense for each project I decide to list.

Audience

My audience will be future employers looking to hire me.

Code + Aesthetics

Hello, I'm Julie

Growing up, technology wasn’t even on my radar—because it didn’t exist as it does now! This perspective gives me a unique edge: I understand the needs of middle-aged and older clients who may feel technology can be daunting, while also keeping up with the latest trends and skills younger generations seek in web design and functionality.

With a passion for both code and aesthetics, I create beautifully functional websites that blend creativity with technical expertise. I thrive in bringing visually appealing ideas to life through code and enjoy working closely with clients to make their visions a reality.

My background as a tech specialist in an educational setting has honed my skills in troubleshooting, problem-solving, and preventative maintenance, which translates into delivering reliable and efficient digital solutions. If a challenge arises, I’m committed to finding a solution, leveraging my strong work ethic, creativity, and technical expertise.

Skills and Expertise:

  • Skill Sets and Languages: HTML, CSS, Wordpress, Joomla, Elementor, network filtering, administrative chromebook management, administative user management, computer management including installing, fixing and maintaining computers (hardware and software), SMARTBoard management (hardware and software), social media for education, newsletters for educational instututions.
  • Website Design and Development: I’ve created responsive websites for local businesses, ensuring compatibility across all devices—crucial for enhancing business visibility and accessibility online.
  • Client-Centered Approach: I prioritize open communication and actively collaborate with clients to understand their business needs and vision, providing guidance on user-friendly, visually appealing designs.
  • User Experience (UX) Design: My focus is on creating seamless, intuitive experiences for users. I work with clients to organize navigation and structure for optimal usability, ensuring every website is easy to navigate and engaging.
  • Cost-Efficiency: I design sites with maintenance simplicity in mind, allowing clients to manage updates independently and save on long-term costs.
  • Adaptable Services: Whether you need a fully customized site, a professional logo, or just a few enhancements, I’m here to help at every step.

I’m known for my integrity, strong communication skills, and dedication to quality. Let’s work together to bring your ideas to life through beautiful, functional web design that meets your goals and exceeds your expectations.
